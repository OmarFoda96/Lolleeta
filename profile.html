About

Hi! My name is Tomy Rivero and I have a strong, diverse background as a celebrity beauty expert. My work ranges from red carpet and beauty campaigns to television and high-end events. I collaborate with Fashion Week every season so I am on top of all of the latest trends. My makeup and hair have been featured in magazines like Instyle, New Beauty, Marie Claire, Life & Style, and Health. I have a love and obsession for everything beauty + fashion and natural looks that emphasize real beauty are my specialty.
